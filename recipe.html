Brownie

One of the most delicious desserts. Crispy, but at the same time soft inside and melts in the mouth.

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C
  2. Melt butter and chocolate
  3. Mix eggs, sugar and salt
  4. Add the cooled chocolate-butter mixture to the egg and mix.
  5. Add floyr and mix until smooth
  6. Pour the dough into the mold
  7. Bake 20-30 min

Cinnamon Roll

These are classic, homestyle cinnamon rolls. The most cozy bakery product.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Whole milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Filling:

  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients together
  2. Warm the milk and butter together
  3. Whisk in the yeast
  4. Pour this mixture over the dry ingredients
  5. Add the egg and then mix everything together
  6. Knead the dough by hand for 3 minutes until a soft dough forms
  7. Roll out the dough and then top with softened butter and the brown sugar & cinnamon mixture
  8. Roll up the dough and then use your sharpest knife to cut into 10-12 rolls

Cake "Potato"

It is a quick and delicious dessert without baking. It's easy and quick to cook!

Ingredients:

  • Cookies 'for coffee'
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Cacao powder
  • Egg
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Grind the cookies into crumbs
  2. Mix with sugar, cacao and milk
  3. Add butter
  4. Add the mixture to the crushed cookies
  5. Form round or oval cakes
